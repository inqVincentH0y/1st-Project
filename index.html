This is a simple recipe for a protein shake. This is great for before, between, or after a workout. With just a few, simple ingredients from the pantry, you can create this protein-filled shake that's perfect to have at any time of the day!

  • 100 grams of old-fashioned oats
  • 1 banana
  • 200g of greek yogurt
  • 30g of peanut butter
  • 300ml of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of honey

    • Blend all ingredients together in a blender and enjoy!
